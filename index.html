About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taipei University of Technology. My research focuses on fiber optic sensors, meta-learning, AI for health applications, and trustworthy machine learning systems. I actively collaborate with international researchers on multi-disciplinary sensing and prediction systems.

I'm actively looking for postdoc and research opportunities starting in 2025. Please feel free to contact me at minyechil21@gmail.com.
