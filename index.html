Biography

I am a doctoral researcher at NTUT, Taiwan, with a strong academic foundation in fiber optics and artificial intelligence. My work explores intelligent sensing systems, particularly for healthcare and smart monitoring.

My interests span signal processing, deep learning architectures, and the optimization of optoelectronic systems. I have also contributed to projects on disease prediction and speech synthesis for underrepresented languages.
